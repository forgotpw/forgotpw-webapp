Recommended Usage

For additional security, we recommend the following way to use our service. When you setup your password at any website you can store a part of that password with ForgotPW which we can send to your phone via text message whenever you need it. The other part of the password should be something common you remember in your head, the combination of which makes the full password. For example, say for Amazon.com you store "happykittens" as your password part with ForgotPW, and remember "11AA!!" which you add to the end of all your passwords. Your full password for Amazon becomes "happykittens11AA!!".


In this way you are not sharing your full password with anyone, and you still only have to remember one password.

Choose your common password part carefully! For this system to work this is not something you can frequently change. It is probably best to include all the characters which some websites require in passwords such as a capital letter, a number, and a special character.
